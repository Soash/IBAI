The Institute of Bioinformatics & Artificial Intelligence (IBAI), an entity of Dawn of Bioinformatics Limited, Bangladesh, offers a robust platform designed to propel you toward a successful career in the burgeoning fields of Bioinformatics and Artificial Intelligence. We have structured a carefully designed 4-Step Launchpad that guides learners from beginner to expert level. These programs commence with Short Courses (covering Genomics, Proteomics, ML & AI) to solidify fundamental concepts for beginners. This is followed by comprehensive Training Programs (25+ programs, available both online and offline) dedicated to enhancing practical skills. For career advancement, we offer a 6-Month Research Internship focused on tools and techniques, preparing graduates and professionals for jobs or higher studies. Finally, our tailored Thesis Support (4-6 months) provides MSc students with personalized one-to-one sessions and essential technical guidance to ensure timely completion. Join IBAI today and empower your future in Bioinformatics and AI.
